### What It Is

Claude Code for Builders is a free, interactive AI course built inside Claude Code — the terminal-based AI tool from Anthropic. Instead of watching videos about AI, you use the tool to build real deliverables for your real business.

The course has 10 modules and takes 4–6 hours. When you finish, you'll have a lean canvas, a 90-day financial model, and a 5-minute pitch — built on your actual business, not a case study.

### How It Works

**1. Clone the repo.**
Download the course materials from GitHub. One command.

**2. Open Claude Code.**
Type `claude` in the terminal. The tool reads the course structure automatically.

**3. Type `/start-0-0`.**
Claude asks you four questions about your business and builds your context file. The course begins.

**4. Work through 10 modules.**
Each module teaches one skill, applied to your real business. Claude guides you step by step.

---

### The Three Agents

In Module 1.4, you run your lean canvas through three AI agents simultaneously:

- **(💰) The Investor** — Stress-tests your model. Asks what a real investor asks before you're in the room.
- **(🧑) The Customer** — Speaks from your target user's perspective. Tests whether this would actually change their behavior.
- **(⚔️) The Competitor** — Maps who already exists. Finds where you're exposed and where you're defensible.

You also build a fourth agent tuned to your specific business and industry.
