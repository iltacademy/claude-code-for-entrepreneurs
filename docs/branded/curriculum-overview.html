The Three Built-In Agents
💰
The Investor
Stress-tests the business model. Asks about unit economics, market size, and competitive moat. Makes founders answer the hard questions before they're in the room.
🧑
The Customer
Speaks from the end user's perspective. Tests whether the solution would actually change behavior — not just sound interesting. Asks "would I pay for this?"
⚔️
The Competitor
Maps who already exists in the space. Names real companies. Identifies where the founder's position is exposed and where it's defensible.
