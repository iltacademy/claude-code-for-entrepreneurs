## The Problem

**What problem are you solving?**
[In plain language. Not "we optimize" or "we leverage." What is broken today that you want to fix?]

[Is this a vitamin (nice to have) or a painkiller (have to have)? How do you know?]

[Even early conversations count. What surprised you?]
